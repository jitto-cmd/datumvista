How We Work

Methodologies we master

The right methodology depends on the question. We advise on and execute across the full spectrum — often combining approaches within a single programme.

CAPI

Computer Assisted Personal Interviewing

Tablet-based face-to-face surveys with real-time data validation, GPS tracking, and quality control — replacing pen-and-paper with accuracy.

CATI

Computer Assisted Telephone Interviewing

Structured telephone surveys using trained callers with scripted questionnaires — ideal for large sample sizes and national coverage.

IDI

In-Depth Interview

One-to-one structured or semi-structured interviews lasting 30–90 minutes, conducted in-person or remotely, with expert moderators.

FGD

Focus Groups

Facilitated group discussions of 6–10 participants for exploratory research, concept testing, and consumer sentiment studies.

Online

Online Surveys & Panels

Self-administered digital surveys with panel recruitment, logic branching, quota management, and multi-device compatibility.

F2F

Pen & Paper Field Surveys

Traditional paper-based data collection for environments where digital tools are not appropriate — with rigorous manual QC processes.
