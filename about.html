Our Foundation

Datumvista Research Ltd is a specialist market research firm with over a decade of fieldwork experience, formally established as a UK-registered company in 2024.

Our founder built this business beginning with customer experience evaluations for major retail brands and later leading large-scale survey operations across the GCC and beyond.

From mapping every cold store across an entire country to interviewing HNIs with USD 5M+ in assets, we bring practitioner-level expertise to every project.
