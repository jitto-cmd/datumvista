Our Foundation

Who we are

Datumvista Research Ltd is a specialist market research firm with over a decade of fieldwork experience, formally established as a UK-registered company in 2024.

Our founder built this business from the ground up — beginning with customer experience evaluations for major retail brands, progressing through partnerships with internationally recognised research firms, and later leading large-scale survey operations across the GCC and beyond using face-to-face, telephone, and digital methodologies.

From mapping every cold store and mini-market across an entire country, to interviewing individuals with USD 5M+ in assets, to running footfall studies in major commercial properties and government-commissioned ministerial programmes — we bring practitioner-level expertise, not just process.
